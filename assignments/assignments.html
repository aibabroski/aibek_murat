Final: Public Service Announcement

A video assignment for Writing for Digital Media.

Write-Up

I. Introduction

Most of the students of Nazarbayev University live in the dormitory on the campus of the university and cook their own food. The kitchen is the most necessary place for cooking. But sometimes students do not clean and throw garbage on the kitchen table, the sink is overflowing and do not clean after cooking. However, some kitchens (small, according to surveys) are clean thanks to the floor leader and the daily duties of students to maintain cleanliness and comfort in the kitchen. It is difficult to control and find strict managers who will maintain cleanliness in the kitchen. We chose this topic because in our situation the kitchen is usually dirty, and on the floor there are mainly boys who sometimes ignore cleaning up after themselves and act at their own discretion.

II. Explanation of problem,solution and advocacy

This kind of problem is quite serious and students don't like it because who would want to cook their own food in dirty and untidy places? According to our survey conducted in the middle summer term of 2025, 56.1% of students said their kitchens were often dirty, mostly from block D8. Block D7 and D9 have strict rules and regulations to control these kinds of issues, but they are not perfect as there are currently no floor leaders, even in the fall and spring semesters the kitchen is usually not cleaned as most students ignore even the block leaders. To solve this problem, DSS and block leaders should monitor and pay more attention to students being able to cook and eat comfortably. Block managers and DSS should start recruiting floor leaders now and in the coming semesters and recruit them for real, not as volunteers. Based on a recruitment survey students should write their qualities and motivation why they want to be floor leader and after that there will be an interview to pick good floor leaders.
