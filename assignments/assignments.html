Midterm: Digital Narrative

A video assignment for Writing for Digital Media.

Write-Up

Overview of the Story

Story about a 13 years old Aibek (myself) who wants to win a gold medal on several subject olympiads for high school students.There were many competitions between students because they cannot go to the olympiads because of olympiad rules like only 1 or 2 students from each region or etc. When Aibek was studying in 8th grade there was a regional olympiad and instead of Aibek, the teacher chose another student (Torekhan) who was the best among his group. Aibek understood that he is weaker after this situation and started to work harder to reach his goals and dreams. In the second half of 2018, there was a Republican Junior Olympiad for 7-8 grades. Aibek saw his opportunity and after his hard work he managed to win a quota for the olympiad after several selection exams made by this teacher. He participated in this competition and won a gold medal at the end. At that time no one believed that Aibek was gonna win this competition because there were a lot of stronger students but still he managed to win first place. This inspiring story of resilience shows that hard work turns dreams into victories in the competitive academic Olympiads.

Connection to the Themes

The story mostly connects with man vs society by showing Aibek’s struggle against rules and competitions in olympiads. As a 13 years old student this might be hard challenges having so many goals and dreams in academic successes. Another student’s selection (Torekhan) was cast as a symbol of social judgment and the teacher said that Aibek was not worthy ("I don't believe in you” on video) of the spot olympiad because Aibek could lower the school's rating. So, Aibek’s response - channeling his disappointment into unbelievable preparation and earning a spot in the Republican Junior Olympiad through selection exams - represents a quiet rebellion against these limitations and resilience. By winning a gold medal in the olympiad he shows that he is worthy and has a lot of potential to win more.

Video Production Techniques

For the video most shots were MS and LS also some Focus changes to show some methods of using a variety of video shooting. All scenes were shot on iPhone 15 Pro using only Cinematic. Video starts with LS with a changing focus where Aibek walks and sees the medals and diplomas of the school's achievements and wants to be here and win a gold medal. After that, M2S where Aibek and his friend went to have some coffee and discuss how Aibek wants to win gold and his friend cheers up and motivates him. In the classroom Aibek and his teacher discuss the next Regional Olympiad in winter and MS shot of both where the teacher chooses another student for the olympiad following a little CU of Aibek’s frustration. After this, XLS of how Aibek is depressed and walking to his room. On the elevator Back MS how he walks then MS enters the room, MS how he is depressed and throws his backpack in a messy room and lies down which shows that he is lazy that he even can't clear his room which also shows this affects academic successes. After that right MS shot where he sits and thinking about his dream and gold. There is HS on Aibek's head showing his work desk and decides to throw away his periphery and takes the note and book to start studying. From the left angle side with MS he started studying and he is still depressed but he can overcome his depression and frustration to achieve his own dream and goals. After this, there are several shots with MS and CU of the teacher and M2S of Aibek and his friend. Following this, after the selection exams there is M2S of the teacher and Aibek, discuss the selection exam’s result and Aibek won top 1 spot to the Junior Republic Olympiad which will be held in Astana. Also in conversation there is a word “çalış” which the teacher said and translated from Turkish is “Work Harder” where we can see that the teacher believes in Aibek and he can make it. By using some shots from other news videos where we can see that the Olympiad is going on and Aibek is writing through the olympiad lab exams and after this M2S of Aibek, the Olympiad organization director giving him his earned gold medal. At the end of the video, there are 2 photos that were shooted in the real olympiad where Aibek won only gold from the Zhambyl Region team.

Creation Process and Challenges

Video edited by Microsoft ClipChamp which is a lightweight app for editing videos faster but the app is not optimized or has some major lags. But still editable and has a lot of ready stuff like videos, audios and animations etc. Editing took like 5-6 hours because of a lack of skills and practice on editing videos so this is time consuming work. Other problems like lack of actors and some ideas to make it a perfect story video for this assignment. Using the minimalism of audios and sound effects is an excellent idea for me because short videos will have so much noise pollution and will not be good enough for expert or high level of editing. Heart beating and depression music were the best choices to show Aibek’s emotional state and having a good conversation between teacher and Aibek is also a good choice. Lack of Olympiad space and vibe gave it some challenges and some labs were closed. Because of this we decided to get a ready video from the news to show that Aibek is participating in the Olympiad. Most news don’t use English language and we decided to add subtitles. At the end 2 photos from the real event was one of the crucial moments where Aibek is confident and happy that winning his first gold medal from the major olympiad in Kazakhstan.
