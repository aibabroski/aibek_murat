Who I Am

I am Aibek Murat, a passionate Chemistry teacher with a deep interest in mobile and web development. My career began in education, where I cultivated a love for teaching complex scientific concepts in an engaging way. Over time, my curiosity for technology led me to explore programming, resulting in projects like "aibachem," which blends my expertise in chemistry with innovative digital solutions. I aim to inspire students and tech enthusiasts alike by creating tools that make learning interactive and accessible.

My background includes a degree in Chemistry Education, supplemented by self-taught skills in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and mobile app development. I thrive at the intersection of science and technology, constantly seeking ways to merge these fields to enhance educational experiences.
